How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your AI GoalGrid account in one of the following ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the AI GoalGrid app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, email us at:

with the subject:
"Account Deletion Request – AI GoalGrid"

Please include:

  • Your registered email
  • Reason (optional)

What Data Is Deleted

  • User profile
  • Study history
  • Goals and progress
  • Associated account data

Data deletion is permanent and completed within 7 days.
